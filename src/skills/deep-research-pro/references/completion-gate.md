# Completion Gate

The task is complete only when **all** of the following are true.

- The main question is answered directly.
- Major claims are backed by **3 or more independent sources** (per `references/verification-policy.md`).
- Important contradictions were identified and addressed.
- A **dedicated contradiction-seeking pass** was completed (per `references/contradiction-seeking-pass.md`).
- Uncertainty is called out explicitly (no smoothing).
- The report includes the **Methodology**, **Contradictions & Contested Claims**, and **Limitations** sections.
- The report is organized for decision-making, not as a raw dump.

## If blocked

If the Ralph loop terminates before the gate passes, the report must end with:

- **What you verified** — the firm conclusions reached.
- **What remains uncertain** — gaps, contested claims, and unresolved contradictions.
- **The minimum next action required** — what a follow-up job would need to do to close the gap.

A blocked-but-honest report is more useful than a smooth-but-overreaching one.
